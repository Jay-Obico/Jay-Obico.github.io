JOSE, JOHN ANTHONY C.
Assistant Professor
Electronics And Communications Engineering
Engr. John Anthony Jose received a degree of BS/MS in Electronics Engineering ladderized program in De La Salle University last 2015. In his graduation, he received Cum Laude, Outstanding master's thesis award, and Eduardo Cojuangco Jr. Award. On his licensure examination, he received an award for being "Top 3 in the ECE board examination" held in October 2015. As a fresh graduate, he worked in Analog Devices as Applications Development Engineer but later realized that teaching and research is his passion. At present, he is teaching various ECE courses and an active member of IEEE Philippine section.
