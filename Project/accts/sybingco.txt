SYBINGCO, EDWIN
Full Professor
Electronics And Communications Engineering
Dr. Edwin Sybingco is a faculty of the DLSU ECE Department. He completed his Doctoral of Philosophy in Electronics and Communications Engineering in De La Salle University area of Digital Signal Processing (DSP) focusing on Big Data and Intelligent Systems. He received his Master of Science (Electronics and Communications Engineering) from De La Salle University. He already published more than 30 scientific papers internationally in the field of DSP, Machine Vision, Computational Intelligence, and robotics.
