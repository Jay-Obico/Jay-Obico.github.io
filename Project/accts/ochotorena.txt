OCHOTORENA, CARLO NOEL E.
Associate Professor
Electronics And Communications Engineering
Carlo Noel Ochotorena received his B.S. and M.S. degrees in Electronics and Communications Engineering from De La Salle University, Manila, Philippines in 2009 and 2012 respectively. He received his Ph.D. in International Development Engineering from the Tokyo Institute of Technology, Japan in 2020 with his research focusing on image filtering techniques. Since 2013, he has been an assistant professor with the Electronics and Communications Engineering at De La Salle University, Manila Philippines. His research interests lie in image processing, image compression, and computational photography. Apart from his main area of research, he also takes an interest in food chemistry with an emphasis towards bringing technology to better understand and enrich existing traditional processes.
