AFRICA, AARON DON M.
Full Professor
Electronics And Communications Engineering
Aaron Don M. Africa earned his B.S. Degree in Electronics and Communications Engineering (ECE) at the University of Santo Tomas. He completed his Masters Degree in ECE at the De La Salle University Manila. Presently, he is an Associate Professor at De La Salle University Manila and A Ph.D. graduate in ECE in said school. His research interest are Control Systems, Artificial Intelligence, Optimization Techniques and Expert Systems.
