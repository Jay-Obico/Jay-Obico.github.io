ALIPIO, MELCHIZEDEK I.
Associate Professor
Electronics And Communications Engineering
He is an associate professor in the Electronics and Communications Engineering Department at De La Salle University. A registered Electronics Engineer in the Philippines, he earned his B.S. in Electronics and Communications Engineering from Colegio de San Juan de Letran Calamba in 2010, his M.E. in Electronics Engineering from Mapua University in 2013, and his Ph.D. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from the University of the Philippines Diliman in 2018. He is currently a member of the National Research Council of the Philippines, Internet Society, Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, and Association of Computing Machinery. He received the IEEE GCCE Best Paper Award in 2017, the Pacific Telecommunications Council (PTC) Young Scholar Award and World Federation of Engineering Organization (WFEO) Young Engineers Laureate Award both in 2018. He received several fellowship awards from the Internet Engineering Task Force, Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers, and Asia Pacific Regional Internet Conference on Operational Technologies since 2016. His research interests are broadly in Wireless Sensor Networks, Software-Defined Networking, Data Analytics in Sensor Networks and Intelligent Systems. He is also an advocate of Internet technology and policymaking.
