MANINGO, JOSE MARTIN Z.
Assistant Professor
Electronics And Communications Engineering
Engr. Jose Martin Z. Maningo is a Teaching Associate in the Department of Electronics and Communications Engineering. He graduated BS-MS ECE Cum Laude in De La Salle University - Manila last 2016. He has placed 10th in the ECE Licensure Exam in April 2017. He has various publications in the field of Swarm Robotics and Computational Intelligence.
