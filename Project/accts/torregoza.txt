TORREGOZA, MARK LORENZE D.
Assistant Prof. Lecturer
Electronics And Communications Engineering
Mark Lorenze Torregoza finished his Bachelor of Science in Electronics and Communications Engineering at Polytechnic University of the Philippines year 2008 and passed the corresponding licensure exam the same year. In 2012 he graduated Master of Science of the same course at De La Salle University Manila and is currently taking his PhD in the same university of the same course. He worked as a network engineer for a telecommunications company early in his career and now a member of the faculty of the ECE department of DLSU-Manila. His research interest is mainly on computer networks, routing and switching algorithms.
