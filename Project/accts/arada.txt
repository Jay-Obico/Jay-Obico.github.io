ARADA, GERALD P.
Associate Professor
Electronics And Communications Engineering
Gerald Pacaba ARADA obtained a B.S. in 1997 from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (Metro Manila, Philippines), and an M.Eng. in 2004 from De La Salle University (Metro Manila, Philippines) -- both in Electronics and Communications Engineering. G. P. Arada has been an Instructor at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines between 1997 and 2006, an engineer with Analog Devices, Inc. (Cavite, Philippines) during 2006-2009. Since 2009, he has been an Assistant Professor at De La Salle University, from where he is presently on leave, to pursue a Ph.D. at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. His research interests are in mutual coupling among wire antennas and sensor array signal processing.
