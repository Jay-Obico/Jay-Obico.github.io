MANZANO, ENRIQUE M.
Associate Professor
Electronics And Communications Engineering
Dr. Enrique M. Manzano obtained his Doctor of Philosophy in Electronics and Communications Engineering from the De La Salle University – Manila having obtained both his Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Electrical Engineering with Specialization in Computers from the University of the Philippines, Diliman Quezon City. His research is focused on conducting polymers having developed a novel solar cell, , super capacitors, super capattery, rechargeable batteries, and other energy –storage devices. He is also active in creating active and passive gas sensors and bio-sensors. In instrumentation, he designs and fabricates non-invasive health-monitoring devices, some in collaboration with universities in Taiwan and in the US. Along these work he has published several papers.
